Our GDWC Entry



What is GDWC?

GDWC stands for Game Development World Championships, which is a game development contest. We are submiting an entry to this competition, but we are not yet sure what to call it! If you want to send an email to Dan (daniel@verticeinteractive.co.uk) to give us some ideas for the name, go ahead!



What is the game going to be about?

The game will be a 2D platformer, and you will need to go through levels as a 2D character (suprise suprise)



Will I be able to play it?

Yes! it will be available on our itch page here so you can download and play it as soon as we are allowed to release it!



Will it cost anything?

As it is an entry to this contest, we will release this game completely free of charge! Yay!
